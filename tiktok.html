TikTok Search Experience Design With billions of videos across thousands of topics, TikTok's content ecosystem is a goldmine of user generated knowledge. The challenge arises where users often struggle to find what they need. Beyond basic filters and tabs, how might we deliver context-aware results that truly answer user queries?

Places of Interest

Users frequently begin their discovery journey on TikTok - researching travel spots, restaurants, or services - only to leave the app to complete bookings or purchases. We saw the opportunity to transform TikTok into an end-to-end platform where discovery leads directly to action. My role: Sole designer for feature launch. Scaled initial MVP for 5 distinct POI categories. Iterative improvements on key performance metrics for conversion and content quality.

LLM Search Tools

Using LLMs, we can pull structured information from public sources, enhancing TikTok’s search results with instant, reliable answers, without having to leave the app. My role: Sole designer for feature launch. Established scalable design guidelines for development of future LLM tools in different search scenarios.

Query Request Card

About 20% of searches do not satisfy user's search queries as a result of content gaps. Majority of TikTok's 1b+ users do not post any content, but many love to engage with creators through comments and discussions. We introduced Q&A posts to enable creators to crowdsource answers directly via search results. My role: Led search-side design. Partnered with creation team to align on cross-functional flows.
