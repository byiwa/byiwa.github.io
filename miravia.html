Ecommerce EU Localisation Project Lazada is a leading e-commerce app based in SEA. In 2019, I joined the design revamp project aiming to expand into the European market, beginning with Spain. To ensure our SEA-centric app can succeed in the EU market, we focused on adapting Lazada's user experience to align with the distinct preferences and needs of Spanish consumers. My role: Design owner for core buyer's journey, involving areas pertaining to search and navigation, campaigns & promotions, product information display, and refunds and returns.
Outcomes Within 1st month of launch, Miravia achieved 1M downloads across all platforms, and 4.5x increase in daily active users by 3rd month of launch. Miravia's CSAT score benchmarked against the original Lazada app also saw marked improvement, ranking 2nd among competitor apps as compared to Lazada, which ranked 5th.
