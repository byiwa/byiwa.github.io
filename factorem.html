Creating an on-demand manufacturing dashboard Factorem is a SEA-based third-party manufacturing startup connecting individuals and businesses to manufacturers across the region. I helped build their web dashboard from 0-1 during their start-up phase.
New user experience redesign In SEA, on-demand manufacturing is a largely traditional industry with most manufacturers unsure of how to navigate complex dashboards. User feedback and data tracking indicated that many first-time visitors were dropping off at the onboarding page without even attempting to upload a file.
We hypothesized that many users were unaware of the 'try with sample part' feature, which could reduce user uncertainty by giving them a risk-free environment to explore the platform's features. I revamped the onboarding page with a simpler information hierarchy and reduced the number of decisions to be made at this stage. The redesign helped to increase page conversion by 3.2x, and reduced drop-off rates by nearly half.
